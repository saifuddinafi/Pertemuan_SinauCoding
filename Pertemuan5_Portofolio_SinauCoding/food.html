Our Signature Menu

Salmon Salad with Mix Veggies

Salmon Salad with Mixed Veggies is a light and refreshing dish, ideal for a healthy lunch or dinner. This salad features perfectly cooked salmon, which is either grilled, baked, or pan-seared, and served over a bed of mixed vegetables. The salmon is seasoned with salt, pepper, and a hint of lemon to enhance its natural flavors.

Fried Salmon with Brocolli

Fried Salmon with Broccoli is a delicious and nutritious dish, perfect for lunch or dinner. This meal is prepared with salmon fillets seasoned with salt, pepper, and your choice of herbs such as dill, parsley, or thyme. The salmon is then fried in a pan with olive oil or butter until the skin is crispy and the flesh is perfectly cooked, usually requiring about 4-5 minutes on each side.

Salmon with BBQ Sauce

Salmon with BBQ Sauce is a flavorful and satisfying dish, perfect for a hearty meal. This dish features salmon fillets that are generously coated with a tangy and sweet BBQ sauce, then grilled or baked to perfection.
